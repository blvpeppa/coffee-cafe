BunnyTasks

About Our Project

BunnyTasks is an adorable yet powerful task management application designed to make productivity fun. With our rabbit-themed interface and motivational features, organizing your daily tasks becomes a joyful experience. Jump from task to task with our unique rabbit-inspired productivity system that rewards you with cute animations and progress carrots!

Key Features:

  • Rabbit-themed task organization
  • Motivational carrot reward system
  • Burrow-style project categorization
  • Multi-platform synchronization
  • Adorable achievement badges
